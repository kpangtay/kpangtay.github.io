COURANT
UX/UI Designer | Developer| Website

Courant is a brand creating wireless chargers that are a high-quality solution for maintaining full batteries in your tech products. The design is made with premium pebble-grain Italian leather for a sleek look that seamlessly fits into any decor style.

Worked on several different updates including adding reviews, ratings, monogramming, bundle feature, and cart update. Created mockups and wire-frames for different features added to the site.

VISIT WEBSITE

Shaping the user journey

The site needed an updated cart to make purchasing more efficient. We wanted to make the experience as simple as possible for the user. The solution was to make purchasing as few clicks as possible and removing any unecessary steps. Instead of a cart drawer we replaced it with a full page experience.

The site also needed a bundle feature so a bundle specific page was created. To make purchasing multiple products one click versus having to add each product individually. Videos explaining how the bundle system works were added at the bottom to help with ease of use of the product.

A new feature that was added was the ability to monogram a product. The solution: adding a module directly on the product page for ease of customization.
