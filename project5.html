COURANT
UI/UX Designer | Developer| Website

Courant is a brand creating wireless chargers that are a high-quality solution for maintaining full batteries in your tech products. The design is made with premium pebble-grain Italian leather for a sleek look that seamlessly fits into any decor style.

As part of the in-house development team I worked on several different updates and additions across the site. I led the end to end design and development of the monogramming, bundle feature and cart update. From discovery, research, exploration, ideation, user testing, wire-framing, mockups, visual design, and prototyping. I collaborated with the development director and various team leads to identify the user needs and visuals.

VISIT WEBSITE

The bundle feature and cart update were both added because the user before had to go through multiple steps before purchasing or even finding a product. The update in the end streamlined the process for the user and removed high friction points for the shoppers. All of the additions and updates to the site rendered an increase in sales and user interaction.

These projects were all challenging and fun as we were aiming at creating a unique digital experience that is also focused on maximizing e-commerce efficiency and revenue.
