I love everything to do with visual and digital design. I have been creating beautifully designed interfaces and experiences for over 5 years. PROJECTS

    Funimation Redesign

    While Funimation earned its reputation on the back of broadcast and home video, this major website overhaul brings the premier anime localization company into the Netflix era. By integrating a modern streaming service with hubs for conversation and an online store, fans of the medium have multiple ways to engage with their favorite titles all on one site.

    Galacticode

    Galacticode is a platform for practicing foundational JavaScript concepts in a 3D gaming interface that guides players through increasingly difficult coding challenges. Players help Nova, a lost alien in space, get home by solving coding challenges to gain fuel points and unlock planets, all while practicing their JavaScript skills.
