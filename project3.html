SPEED RACER
UI/UX Designer | Design Lead | Website

Speed Racer is one of the most iconic shows of the 60s and 70s that has captivated audiences from every corner of the globe. Celebrating the 50th anniversary of the series, the Collectorâ€™s Edition will come complete with Speed Racer, Mach Go Go Go, and Mach Go Go Go: Restart in the first-ever official U.S. release of the Japanese audio. I created a website design to promote the sale of Speed Racer The Ultimate Collection - Collector's Edition. My concept was selected from amongst my peers.

As the lead designer on the site I wanted the design to have a retro feel to appeal to the nostalgic nature of the product. I did research on the show and the age of the audience to whom this product would most appeal. I knew how important and impactful the show was to fans so I wanted the site to be authentic to the look and feel of the original show. After a few initial designs I did a user study for the specific age demographic to see if it would appeal to them. After the design was selected I then started creating wireframes for the site that would allow for a simple responsive grid across all dimensions. I handed off the designs to the development team and worked closely with them to make sure everything was seamless. I updated the design for each new phase of the campaign in accordance with marketing goals.
